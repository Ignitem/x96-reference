MOV

Move data from a location to another.

Encoding

OpcodeInstructionOperand encodingDescription
B8 /0mov reg, immRegAddr:ImmSize:ImmValMove imm to reg
B7 /0mov reg2, reg1RegAddr:RegAddrMove reg1 to reg2
B6 /0mov reg, memRegAddr:MemAddrMove mem to reg
B5 /0mov mem, immMemAddr:ImmSize:ImmValMove imm to mem
B4 /0mov mem, regMemAddr:RegAddrMove reg to mem
B3 /0mov mem2, mem1MemAddr:MemAddrMove mem1 to mem2
